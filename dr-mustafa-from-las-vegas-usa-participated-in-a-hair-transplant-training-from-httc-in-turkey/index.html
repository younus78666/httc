Dr. Mustafa from Las Vegas, USA, participated in a Hair transplant training from HTTC in Turkey

Home/Blog/Dr. Mustafa, Las Vegas

Are you a physician interested in starting your hair restoration business? Do you want to offer the best possible quality to your patients? Look no further than the Hair Transplant Training Center (HTTC) in Turkey Ankara, where you can train on live patients and attend hair restoration courses with long-experienced FUE hair transplantation experts.

Triple board-certified physician, Dr. Mustafa from Las Vegas, USA, knows the value of investing in education. That's why he traveled all the way to Ankara, Turkey, to do his one-week training at HTTC with the best in hair transplant surgery.

At HTTC, you'll use state-of-the-art tools from Cole instruments and learn all techniques, from consultation to post-op care. But what sets this training apart from others is the opportunity to work on live patients with the help of experts. No workshop or book can teach you the secrets of hair transplant surgery like this training can.

Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, HTTC's hair restoration courses are tailored to fit your needs. With the hands-on training and guidance of FUE hair transplantation experts, you'll be well-equipped to provide the best possible quality to your patients.

Investing in your education is investing in your future. Contact HTTC for more information and take the first step toward success in the field of FUE hair transplantation surgeries."
