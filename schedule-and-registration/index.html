Schedule, pricing and registration

The HTTC Hair Transplant Training Center in Ankara offers flexible scheduling for doctors, physicians and hair surgeons at every experience level. Training sessions are arranged individually to match your availability and learning objectives.

Training Formats

One-on-One Training – Dedicated hands-on instruction with direct supervision from our senior surgeons. You work on real patients from day one. Ideal for focused skill development at your own pace.

Small Group Workshops – 2 to 4 participants per session. Collaborative learning environment with ample hands-on time for each participant. Benefit from observing different cases and techniques across the group.

Duration ranges from intensive 1-day courses to comprehensive 4-week programs, depending on your experience level and goals.

Pricing

HTTC training costs USD 5,000 per day. This includes:

  • Hands-on practice on real patients under direct surgeon supervision
  • Theoretical instruction (hair anatomy, donor management, hairline design)
  • Instrument training including the PCID from Cole Instruments
  • All surgical tools, equipment and consumables
  • Certificate of completion signed by HTTC instructors
  • Accommodation assistance available

What Makes HTTC Different

Most hair transplant training programs worldwide charge thousands of dollars for observation only, you watch, but never touch a patient. HTTC is one of the only training centers globally where participants perform extractions and implantations themselves on real patients, under direct supervision of surgeons with over 20 years of FUE experience.

Turkey is one of the few countries where hands-on training on real patients is legally permitted, providing practical surgical experience that cadaver-based or simulation-only courses simply cannot match.
