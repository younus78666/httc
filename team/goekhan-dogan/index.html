About Goekhan Dogan

Gökhan Dogan, Manager of the Hairline Clinic and Hair Transplant Training Center

Professional management and consulting are within the daily spectrum of Goekhan Dogan. The manager of the Hairline Clinic and the Hair Transplant Trainingcenter has intensively focused on strategic concepts to cooperate with international clinics and hair loss doctors in order to ensure high standards for hair restoration worldwide.

Goekhan Dogan has developed reliable business cooperation on mutual trust with a number of famous and noted hair surgeons / physicians in the beauty industry. Achieving essential knowledge and particular expertise through clinic visits worldwide and a regular attendance at important international congresses Mr. Dogan has become an expert for the organization of internationally based training courses and workshops.
