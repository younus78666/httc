On completion

Hair Transplant Training Certificate

Every participant who completes an HTTC Hair Transplant Training Course or FUE Workshop receives a formal completion certificate, signed by the instructing hair surgeon and documenting the techniques practiced, the supervised cases observed or performed, and the total training hours completed in Ankara, Turkey.

View the training course → FUE Workshop
Anatomy of the certificate

What the certificate confirms.

Signed completion certificate · Ankara, Turkey
  1. 01

    Participant identity

    and professional role (physician, surgeon, dermatologist)

  2. 02

    Training format

    : Course (1-5 days) or Workshop (1-2 days)

  3. 03

    Techniques covered

    : manual FUE extraction, stick-and-place implantation, PCID operation, hairline design, donor-area evaluation, graft handling

  4. 04

    Case exposure

    : number of live patient cases observed or participated in during the training

  5. 05

    Instructor confirmation

    : signed by Dr. Özgür Öztan (Medical Director, HTTC) or the lead instructor of the session

  6. 06

    Date and location

    : Ankara, Turkey, with exact training dates

Professional recognition
ISHRS
1,000+ physician members worldwide

Recognised professional development.

HTTC certificates document professional development (CME-style) but are not a substitute for formal fellowship accreditation. Several of our instructing surgeons are members of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS), the leading global authority on hair restoration with more than 1,000 physician members worldwide. The hands-on case exposure gained at HTTC is frequently cited by attending physicians in applications to hair-restoration societies, continuing-education portfolios, and clinic credentials.

Put it to work

How physicians use the HTTC certificate.

01

Clinic credentialing:

document your hair-transplant training to a clinic or hospital employer

02

Insurance and liability:

demonstrate verifiable technique exposure if required by your local regulatory body

03

Patient-facing credentials:

display the certificate in your consultation room or website to build patient trust

04

CME evidence:

submit the certificate as continuing-education evidence where your jurisdiction accepts international training

05

Society-membership applications:

supplement ISHRS or regional-society application packages with documented hands-on hours

International acceptance

Alumni from more than 20 countries.

Physicians from more than 20 countries have completed HTTC training and are currently practicing hair restoration using the techniques taught in Ankara. Our alumni include surgeons from Germany, Switzerland, Austria, the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, Brazil, Mexico, Qatar, India and across the EU. The acceptance of the HTTC certificate as evidence of training depends on your own regulatory framework, we recommend verifying with your medical board before booking.

Issue and verify

Your copies and verification.

Digital PDF copy

Digital and physical copy

Each certificate is issued in two formats: a signed physical copy handed to you at the end of your training, and a digital PDF copy emailed to the address you provided during enrolment. The PDF version is watermarked and includes a verification code that allows third parties (employers, societies, regulatory bodies) to confirm the training authenticity directly with HTTC upon request.

Signed physical copy

Verification

To verify a previously issued HTTC certificate, employers and societies may contact us at info@hairtransplant-trainingcenter.com with the participant name, training date and certificate verification code. We respond to verification requests within 2 business days.
