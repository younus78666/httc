About the Hair Transplant Training Center

Designed for modern hair transplant training.

Designed for modern hair transplant training, the Hair Transplant Training Center in Ankara offers a series of workshops and training courses to attending hair loss physicians and surgeons who want to become leading experts in the field.

The center provides instruction on practical instruments, medical tools and modern surgical techniques for treating hair loss. It regularly presents leading doctors from the worldwide hair transplant industry, who deliver expert courses and workshops with real practice on patients.

Every programme is structured for physicians and medical assistants, and it's led by internationally recognized instructors who teach the same standard of care they use in their own clinics.

What the center provides

Everything a doctor needs to learn FUE properly.

HTTC covers the practical side of hair restoration that conferences leave out: the instruments, the technique and the patient.

  • 01

    Practical instruments and medical tools

    Hands-on instruction on the punches, implanters and devices behind a safe FUE procedure.

  • 02

    Modern surgical techniques

    Current techniques for treating hair loss, taught the way they're actually practised.

  • 03

    Practice on real patients

    Supervised work on live cases, so technique is learned in the room, not from slides.

  • 04

    International instructors

    Leading doctors from the worldwide hair transplant industry lead each session.

  • 05

    Workshops and full courses

    A regular series of formats matched to your level and clinical objectives.

Who founded HTTC

Founded by an international group of hair restoration experts.

The Hair Transplant Training Center was founded by experienced surgeons and clinicians from the United States and Turkey, in cooperation with Hairforlife in Switzerland. Together they shaped a single, practical standard for training the next generation of FUE doctors, with Dr. Özgür Öztan leading the clinical faculty in Ankara.
