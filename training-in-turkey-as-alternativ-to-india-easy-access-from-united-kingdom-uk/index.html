Hair Transplant Training in Turkey as alternativ to India: Easy access from United Kingdom (UK) and other countries

Central location of our fue hair transplant training center in Turkey – Ankara and as an training alternative to India. Easy access as from United Kingdom (UK), Germany, Belgium and other countries

The legal provisions in India allow – as well as in Turkey – to train on real patients. Therefore many doctors are asking for a training- course in India.

Easier to organize fue hair transplant training patients in India and Turkey then as example in UK – United Kingdom

Likewise it seems that in India – as in Turkey – it is easier to organize possible training patients as example in United Kingdom (UK, England, Scotland, Wales, Ireland), Germany or other countries in Western Europe. This is probably the reason why many doctors from the UK, Germany, Belgium or other countries are searching for hair restoration training (courses) in india.

HTTC – Hair Transplant Training Center in Turkey is located in the southeast of Europe and therefore can be reached very quickly for example by airplane from United Kingdom (UK, England, Scotland, Wales, Ireland), Germany, Belgium and other countries.

Because the good location of the HTTC in Turkey – Ankara may HTTC in future will be a good alternative for all doctors from UK – United Kingdom or other countries in western europe which are interested in a hair transplant and hair restoration training course on real patients and to gain fue hair transplant experience.

Another advantage: Doctors which are interested in the automated, robotic fue extraction machine “PCID” of Cole Instruments can now test the device in the Hair Transplant Training Center in Turkey, instead of having to travel to the US to Atlanta

On this page we already wrote about the PCID of Cole Instruments. After many requests from hair surgeons in automated and robotic fue extraction machines (tools – devices) who want to extract with fue the grafts more fast and save, Cole Instruments developed special for FUE Hair Restoration the PCID device, a automated – robotic fue extraction machine – tool.

Another advantage of the HTTC – Hair Transplant Training Center in Turkey – Ankara: Doctors as example from United Kingdom – Uk or other countries from western europe and which are interested in the automated, robotic fue extraction machine “PCID” of Cole Instruments can now test the device in our Center in Turkey – Ankara, instead of having to travel to the US to Cole Instruments in Atlanta.

Are you interested in hair restoration training courses, in a FUE Workshop, or to test the PCID FUE Extraction tool from Coleinstruments, feel free to contact our Hair Transplant Center in Turkey every time!
