What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser to remember preferences, authenticate sessions, or analyse website usage.

Which cookies do we use?

How to manage cookies

  • Refuse or revoke consent: Delete the httc_consent cookie in your browser, the banner will appear again on your next visit.
  • Block all cookies: Configure your browser to refuse all cookies. Note that this may affect site functionality.
